From commodity ingredient to premium product platform

Built the strategic case for repositioning Omega-3 from a single SKU to a multi-product platform targeting life-stage specific needs, starting with the prenatal segment.

Building a custom PIM from scratch for a mission-critical industrial manufacturer

Replaced a manual stack of ERP exports, spreadsheets, and emails with a custom PIM for 3,000+ SKUs across four regulated industries.

The problem

PrecisionSense relied on ERP for transactional data, but product descriptive and compliance data lived in scattered systems, requiring 20 hours/week of manual gathering. Data update speeds were slow and audit trails didn't exist.

My approach

Led product definition for a custom PIM designed around a "vanilla architecture" principle to minimize external dependencies. Focused on 5 core modules: ETL onboarding, parent-child data model, RBAC and audit trail, asset management, and full export capability.

Turning a cold chain from a liability into a monitored asset

Priya Foods Distribution managed ₹42 lakhs of perishable stock with manual logs. I led the product definition of ColdGuard, an IoT monitoring system that cut failure response time from minutes to seconds.

The problem

A regional distributor relied on manual temperature logs and a slow-to-start generator to protect ₹42 lakhs of perishable stock, resulting in major financial losses from undetected refrigeration failures.

My approach

Led product definition for an IoT monitoring system (ESP32 sensors, AWS, TimescaleDB) to automate temperature logging and create real-time alerting based on sustained condition breaches instead of single spikes.

Approach

How I work

Good product work starts with understanding the problem clearly before reaching for a solution. The rest tends to follow.

01
Start with the problem

User interviews, support tickets, data — everything gets read before a spec is written. I spend more time here than most, because it pays off later.

02
Build the business case

Good ideas don't always get funded. I write honest ROI estimates and clear risk analysis — the kind that make decisions easier, not harder.

03
Ship with the team

Specs engineers want to read, design context that unlocks creativity, blockers cleared before they become fires. Delivery is a team sport.

04
Learn and share

Every launch is a learning opportunity. I document what worked and what didn't so the next team doesn't start from zero.
