01
Product - B2B SaaS

Redesigning the core workflow that cut churn by 40%

Led discovery and redesign of a high-friction workflow for 50K+ daily users. JTBD interviews and session replay pinpointed a 4-step process collapsible to one, shipped in 6 weeks.

DiscoveryRetentionJTBDA/B Testing
The problem

Enterprise accounts churning silently. Session recordings revealed users repeating a 4-step workflow every session, adding 3+ unnecessary minutes of friction per use.

My approach

12 JTBD interviews alongside session replay analysis. Key insight: users weren't confused, they were frustrated. Collapsed 4 steps to 1 with context pre-filling. A/B tested before full rollout.

Outcomes

-40% churn rate - -3 min per session - 94% task completion - shipped in 6 weeks

Methods

JTBD interviews - Session replay - A/B testing - Funnel analysis - Progressive rollout

How I work

My approach

Good product work starts with understanding the problem clearly before reaching for a solution. I spend more time in discovery than most PMs - because clarity in discovery saves weeks in delivery.

01
Start with the problem

User interviews, support data, analytics - everything gets read before a spec is written. I prioritize understanding over velocity in discovery.

02
Build the business case

Good ideas don't always get funded. I write honest ROI estimates and risk analysis - the kind that make decisions easier, not harder.

03
Ship with the team

Specs engineers want to read, design context that unlocks creativity, blockers cleared before they become fires. Delivery is a team sport.

04
Learn and document

Every launch is a learning event. I document what worked and what didn't so the next team doesn't start from zero.
